Our History

Since 1979, MTC Holdings has grown from tiny shop in the Western Cape into a home for all South Africans. We have stayed true to our roots by focusing on what matters, real people.

Mission & Vision

To be the heart of South African retail a place where every person feels seen and every product brings genuine value.We are here to make life little easier. By keeping essentials affordable and quality high, we aim to lift up families and communities we serve through honest thoughtful work.
